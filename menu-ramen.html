Tonkotsu, Ramen Rich and creamy broth made from simmered pork bones, accompanied by tender chashu pork, perfectly cooked noodles, and an assortment of toppings. It's a classic choice that will delight your senses.
Oyakodon (Chicken Egg Rice Bowl) Oyakodon is a Japanese dish that consists of chicken and eggs cooked in a savory broth and served over rice. The name literally means "parent-and-child rice bowl", as it uses both chicken and eggs. Oyakodon is a simple and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed any time of the day.
Spicy Miso, Ramen Robust and savory notes of our broth crafted from fermented spicy soybean paste. Topped with fish cake, bean sprouts, and a sprinkling of green onions, this dish offers a delightful blend of flavors.
Tantanmen, Ramen Indulge in the bold and spicy flavors of our Tantanmen. features a creamy and aromatic sesame-based broth, infused with a perfect balance of chili oil and spices. Topped with succulent chopped of chashu pork, bok choy, half-boiled egg and a sprinkle of green onions.
Tsukemen A dipping-style ramen that takes your taste buds on a flavorful journey. In this delectable dish, the noodles are served separately from the rich and savory broth, allowing you to dip each strand into the flavorful soup before taking a bite.
Abura Soba Abura Soba is served without broth, allowing the toppings and noodles to take center stage. The noodles are tossed in a rich, flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, vinegar, and other secret ingredients, creating a mouthwatering combination of savory and tangy flavors. Topped with a medley of ingredients such as chashu pork, green onions, nori, and a perfectly soft-boiled egg.
