Dessert
Mochi Ice Cream Mochi ice cream is a delicious Japanese dessert that combines soft and chewy rice cakes with creamy ice cream. It comes in a variety of flavors, from classic vanilla and chocolate to exotic mango and green tea. Mochi ice cream is made with sweet rice flour, water, sugar, and cornstarch, which are cooked in the microwave to form a sticky dough. The dough is then cut into circles and wrapped around balls of ice cream. Mochi ice cream is a fun and easy way to enjoy a traditional Japanese treat with a modern twist.
Caramel Pudding Indulge yourself with our caramel pudding, a smooth and creamy dessert topped with a layer of rich caramel sauce. This classic treat is made with fresh milk, eggs and vanilla, and baked to perfection in a water bath. Enjoy it warm or chilled, with whipped cream or fresh fruits on the side.
Anmitsu Anmitsu is a traditional Japanese dessert that consists of small cubes of agar jelly, sweet red bean paste, mochi, various fruits and a drizzle of black sugar syrup. It is usually served in a bowl with a spoon and enjoyed as a refreshing treat on hot days. Anmitsu is also customizable, as you can choose your favorite fruits and toppings to create your own version. Some common additions are ice cream, whipped cream, chestnuts and green tea powder.
Dango Dango is a traditional Japanese dessert made of sweet rice flour balls skewered on bamboo sticks. They are usually served with different types of sauces, such as red bean paste, soy sauce, or green tea. Dango is soft and chewy, and can be enjoyed hot or cold. Dango is a perfect treat for any occasion, whether you want a light snack or a satisfying dessert.
Matcha Pudding Enjoy a refreshing and creamy dessert with our matcha pudding. Made with high-quality matcha powder and fresh milk, this pudding has a smooth texture and a rich flavor. The pudding is topped with whipped cream and red bean paste for a sweet and satisfying finish. Try our matcha pudding today and experience the taste of Japan.
Matcha Mille Crepe Enjoy a slice of our matcha mille crepe cake, a delicate dessert made with thin layers of green tea crepes and smooth whipped cream. Each bite is a harmony of sweet and earthy flavors, with a light and airy texture. This cake is perfect for any occasion, whether you want to impress your guests or treat yourself to something special.
