Hamamatsu Gyoza Mineral water is a type of water that comes from a natural spring and contains various minerals, such as salts and sulfur compounds. depending on whether it has added carbon dioxide or not. Mineral water is often enjoyed for its refreshing taste and health benefits.
Pilsner Urquell Pilsner Urquell is a Czech lager that has been brewed since 1842. It has a golden color, a rich malt flavor, and a distinctive hop aroma. Pilsner Urquell is the original pilsner beer and a symbol of Czech brewing tradition.
Ocha Tea Ocha tea is a term for green tea in Japanese culture. It is a refreshing and enjoyable drink that can be served hot or cold. Ocha tea is made from the first brew of fine tea leaves, which gives it a pleasant taste and aroma. Ocha tea has many health benefits, such as antioxidants, hydration, and mild caffeine.
Draft Beer Asahi Draft Beer is a refreshing and crisp lager brewed with rice and malt. It has a light golden color and a smooth, creamy head that resembles a draft beer served in a mug. Asahi Draft Beer is perfect for pairing with grilled meats and other dishes.
Sochu Sochu is a distilled alcoholic beverage made from rice, barley, sweet potatoes, or other grains. It is a clear and colorless liquid with a mild flavor and a high alcohol content. Sochu is typically served neat, on the rocks, or mixed with water or fruit juice.
Matcha Latte Enjoy a refreshing and energizing drink made with high-quality matcha powder and steamed milk. Matcha is a finely ground green tea that has a rich flavor and many health benefits. Our matcha latte is lightly sweetened and can be served hot or iced.
